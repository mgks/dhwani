Hindustani Swar Tuner

Designed for Indian classical vocalists. It uses Just Intonation relative to a base frequency (Sa = 240Hz). Practice your swars and ensure your intonation is pitch-perfect. Humming is recommended for the best accuracy.

Instrument Tuner

A universal chromatic tuner using Equal Temperament (A4 = 440Hz). Suitable for guitars, violins, cellos, and other acoustic instruments. Watch the needle to adjust your strings.

Metronome

A precise tempo-keeping tool. Adjust the BPM (Beats Per Minute) using the slider or +/- buttons. The visual indicator flashes with each beat to help you stay in time.

Tips for Accuracy

  • Use in a quiet environment.
  • Position your microphone close to the sound source.
  • For vocalists, try sustained humming (Akaar) rather than lyrics for more stable pitch detection.
